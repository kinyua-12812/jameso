Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up as a teacher?

To sign up as a teacher, click on the "I'm a Teacher" button on our homepage. You'll be guided through a simple registration process where you can create your profile, list your qualifications, and set up your teaching preferences. Verification of credentials may take 1-2 business days.

What are the fees for using the platform?

For students, there are no subscription fees—you only pay for the lessons you book. Teachers pay a 15% service fee on each completed lesson, which covers platform maintenance, payment processing, and customer support. We offer discounted rates for teachers with high session volumes.

How are payments processed?

We use secure payment processing through Stripe. Students are charged when they book a session, but funds are only released to teachers after the session is completed. Teachers receive payments weekly via direct deposit to their bank account. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

What technology do I need to teach online?

You'll need a reliable internet connection, a computer or tablet with a webcam, and a quiet space for teaching. Our platform works on all major browsers and includes built-in video conferencing tools. For optimal experience, we recommend using Chrome or Firefox with at least 10Mbps upload/download speeds.

Can I cancel or reschedule a lesson?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule lessons through your dashboard. Please note our cancellation policy: cancellations more than 24 hours before the session receive a full refund, while late cancellations may incur a fee. Teachers can set their own cancellation policies within certain guidelines.
